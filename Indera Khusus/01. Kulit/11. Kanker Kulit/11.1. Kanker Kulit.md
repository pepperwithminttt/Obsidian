# 1. BASAL CELL CARCINOMA (BCC) 🌑

## Definisi

Keganasan dari:
### sel basal epidermis

Paling sering ditemukan (80%)

---
## KARAKTERISTIK BCC 📌

✅ Tumbuh lambat  
✅ Invasif lokal  
✅ Jarang metastasis (<1%)

---
## Sinonim 📚

- Basalioma
- Rodent ulcer
- Ulcus rodens
- Basal cell epithelioma
- Yacobi ulcus
- Kromecher Tumour

### Kenapa disebut “rodent ulcer”? 🐭

Karena ulkus tampak seperti “digigit tikus”.

---
## PATOGENESIS ☀️

Paparan UV kronik → mutasi DNA → proliferasi sel basal.

Gen yang sering:

- PTCH (hedgehog pathway)

---
## MANIFESTASI KLINIS 🔍

### Keluhan:

- tahi lalat membesar
- mudah berdarah
- ulkus tak sembuh

---
## Gambaran khas BCC 📸

### Nodul ulseratif:

✅ papul/nodul mengilat  
✅ tepi meninggi  
✅ tengah ulkus  
✅ teleangiektasis

#### Istilah penting 📚

- **Teleangiektasis** = pelebaran pembuluh darah kecil superfisial.

---
## TIPE-TIPE BCC 📚

| Tipe        | Gambaran                              |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Nodular     | Nodul mengilat, warna seperti mutiara |
| Superfisial | Patch merah berskuama                 |
| Pigmented   | Hitam/coklat                          |
| Morpheaform | Seperti jaringan parut                |

## DIAGNOSIS 🔬

### Biopsi:

#### Punch biopsy

Mengambil sebagian jaringan dengan alat berbentuk silinder.

#### Excision biopsy

Mengangkat seluruh lesi, sebagai diagnosis histopatologikal sekaligus terapi.

---
## TATALAKSANA 💉

### Terapi utama:

#### OPERASI ✂️

##### Wide excision

Dengan safety margin:  
0,5–1 cm.

---
### Teknik operasi:

- Eksisi biasa
- Mohs micrographic surgery
- Cryosurgery ❄️
- Curettage + elektrodesikasi

- Pengangkatan lapis demi lapis sambil diperiksa mikroskopis.
- ==Dipake untuk area yang sempit/sedikit jaringannya== (e.g. cantus, lipatan nasolabial, periorbital, periauricular). Kalo area yang luas kayak batang tubuh dan extremitas itu bisa pake wide excision sja.

Keunggulan:  
✅ margin sangat akurat

# 2. SQUAMOUS CELL CARCINOMA (SCC) 🔥

## DEFINISI

Keganasan dari:
### keratinosit epidermis

Sinonim:
- Planoselulare Carcinoma
- Cell Prickle Carcinoma
- Epidermoid Carcinoma
- Bowen Carcinoma,
- Cornifed Epithelioma

![gh|465](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pepperwithminttt/Obsidian/main/ImagesDump/1778418557000dtzyth.png)

---
## EPIDEMIOLOGI

- The 2nd most common of skin malignancy
- Highest incidence aged 50 – 70 years
- Men >> women
- Most often on colored skin in the tropical countries
- Lesions can arise from normal skin or from pre-cancerous lesions
- Most locations:
	- White Skin : Face, Upper Extremities
	- Black : lower extremities, lower lip, dorsum manus

---
## KARAKTERISTIK 📌

✅ lebih agresif dari BCC  
✅ cepat tumbuh  
✅ mudah metastasis

---
## ETIOLOGI ⚠️

- UV
- arsenik
- tar
- luka bakar kronik
- ulkus Marjolin

---
### Ulkus Marjolin 📚

Keganasan SCC yang muncul pada:
#### luka kronik/luka bakar lama

---
## MANIFESTASI KLINIS 🔍

### Lesi:

✅ eksofitik (“cauliflower”) 🥦  
✅ ulkus  
✅ mudah berdarah  
✅ bau khas

---
### Istilah penting 📚

#### Eksofitik

Tumbuh menonjol keluar.

#### Endofitik

Tumbuh infiltratif ke dalam.

---
## METASTASIS 🚨

SCC dapat menyebar ke:

- kelenjar limfe
- paru
- hati

---
## PEMERIKSAAN PENUNJANG

- Radiology: X-ray of the bone in the lesion area, and CT-Scan/MRI over indication, Thoracic X-ray for metastasis
- Biopsy for histopathological examinations:
	- Lesions < 2 cm: excision biopsy
	- Lesions > 2 cm: incision biopsy

# 3. MELANOMA MALIGNA 🌑⚠️

## DEFINISI

Keganasan dari:
### melanosit

Paling mematikan 😱

- The most unpredictable tumor
- In addition to the skin, it can also occur in the mucosa
- All ages, >> ages 35-55
- Male = Female

---
## FAKTOR RISIKO ⚠️

- Congenital nevi >5% of body surface area -> Risiko transformasi
- Previous history of melanoma -> Risiko rekurensi
- Hereditary factors
- Dysplastic nevi syndrome -> lesi prekanker
- There are 5 nevi with a diameter of >5mm
- There are 50 NEVI with a diameter of >2mm
- History of sun exposure/burn -> kerusakan DNA
- White race, red hair, blue eyes, frecles -> melanin sedikit
- Living in the tropical countries, psoralen sunscreen
- Xeroderma pigmentosum

---
## MANIFESTASI KLINIS 🔍

Keluhan:  
✅ tahi lalat cepat membesar  
✅ gatal  
✅ berdarah  
✅ warna berubah

---
## ABCDE MELANOMA 🧠✨

| Huruf | Arti               |
| ----- | ------------------ |
| A     | Asymmetry          |
| B     | Border irregular   |
| C     | Colour variation   |
| D     | Diameter >6 mm     |
| E     | Elevation/Evolving |

## TIPE-TIPE MELANOMA 📚

|Tipe|Gambaran|
|---|---|
|Lentigo maligna|Pada usia tua|
|Superficial spreading|Paling sering|
|Nodular|Agresif|
|Acral lentiginous|Telapak/kuku|
|Amelanotic|Tidak berpigmen|

---
### Lentigo Melanoma Maligna (LMM)

![gh|233](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pepperwithminttt/Obsidian/main/ImagesDump/17784201300000xgz8e.png)

Lesions: blackish-brown, a few cm, irregular edges, dark spots (blue color) on the surface, scattered irregularly, can be invasive, blackish-blue nodules rather hyperkeratonic.

### Superfisial Spreading Melanoma (SSM)

![gh|237](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pepperwithminttt/Obsidian/main/ImagesDump/17784201480008tygt4.png)

- Lesions: Macula or archiformis plaque;  brown, gray, blue, black, Redness in colour
- It can arise from a pre-existing melanocytic nevus.
- Asymmetric, irregular edges, varied color.

### Nodular Malignant Melanoma (NMM)

![gh|261](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pepperwithminttt/Obsidian/main/ImagesDump/17784202780001wnqzw.png)

- Lesions: Reddish-brown or blue to blackish brown in colour; nodules/papules, ulcerated, in shaping; bleeding, accompanied by squama/crust on the surface.

### Acral Lentiginous Melanoma 👣

![gh|298](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pepperwithminttt/Obsidian/main/ImagesDump/1778420292000yeh1gr.png)

Sering pada:

- telapak
- tumit
- bawah kuku

- Lesions: Macula lesions, brown-black, irregular edges, flesh colored depiction, sometimes resembling clavus, veruka, pyogenic granulomas.

## HISTOPATOLOGI & MIKROSTAGING 🔬

### Clark Level

Menilai:
#### kedalaman invasi anatomis

| Level | Kedalaman                                        |
| ----- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| I     | In situ (sel melanoma ada di atas stratum basal) |
| II    | Invasi ke papillary dermis                       |
| III   | Invasi ke junction papillary-reticular           |
| IV    | Invasi ke reticular dermis                       |
| V     | Invasi ke subkutis                               |

---
### Breslow Thickness 📏

Menilai:
#### ketebalan tumor (mm)

Semakin tebal:  
➡ prognosis makin buruk.
