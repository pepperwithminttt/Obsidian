# 1. 💧 ANATOMI & DEFINISI SISTEM LAKRIMAL

## 📌 Sistem lakrimal

Sistem lakrimal adalah struktur kompleks yang terdiri dari:

### 🔹 Sistem sekresi (pembentuk air mata)

- **Glandula lacrimalis utama** → menghasilkan komponen aqueous tear
- **Glandula aksesori (Krause & Wolfring)**
- **Kelenjar Meibom** → menghasilkan lipid layer

### 🔹 Sistem ekskresi (drainase air mata)

- **Punctum lacrimale**
- **Canaliculus (superior & inferior)**
- **Saccus lacrimalis**
- **Ductus nasolacrimalis → meatus inferior nasi**

💡 Fungsi utama:

- Lubrikasi kornea
- Proteksi terhadap mikroorganisme
- Menjaga stabilitas tear film

# 2. 💥 TRAUMA DUKTUS LAKRIMAL (CANALICULAR LACERATION)

## 📌 Definisi

Trauma pada sistem drainase lakrimal akibat **cedera mekanik mendadak** yang menyebabkan **robekan (laceration)** pada canaliculus.

📍 Lokasi penting:

- Medial palpebra (dekat canthus medialis)

---
## 📊 Epidemiologi

- Kasus relatif jarang (< bagian kecil luka wajah)
- Lebih sering:
    - 👦 laki-laki
    - usia muda (<40 tahun)
- Penyebab tersering:
    - trauma tumpul & tajam
    - gigitan hewan 🐶
    - kecelakaan 🚗

---
## ⚡ Etiologi

### 🔪 Trauma langsung (direct)

→ mengenai langsung canaliculus  
Contoh:
- kaca
- pisau
- kuku
- gigitan hewan

### 🥊 Trauma tidak langsung (indirect)

→ trauma tumpul sekitar orbita
- pukulan
- benturan

---
## ⚠️ Faktor Risiko

- Usia muda (aktivitas tinggi)
- Laki-laki
- Trauma wajah
- Gigitan hewan
- Prosedur operasi sebelumnya
- Trauma saat lahir

---
## 🔬 Patofisiologi

Trauma → robekan jaringan → dapat mengenai:

- kulit
- otot palpebra
- septum orbita
- sistem lakrimal

➡️ Jika tidak diperbaiki:  
→ gangguan drainase → **epiphora kronik** (mata berair terus)

---
## 🧠 Diagnosis

### 🗣️ Anamnesis

- mekanisme trauma
- waktu kejadian
- kemungkinan benda asing
- riwayat mata sebelum trauma

---
### 👁️ Tanda & Gejala

- eritema, edema
- lacerasi horizontal
- lemak preseptal keluar (→ septum robek)
- ptosis (→ levator rusak)
- deformitas canthus medialis

#### 😣 Symptoms:

- nyeri
- perdarahan
- penglihatan kabur
- anestesi supraorbital (mengindikasikan laserasi dekat sudut orbital)

---
## 💊 Tatalaksana

### 🚑 Penanganan awal (FIRST AID)

- Karena sering karna trauma, lakukan DRSABCD

- jangan tekan bola mata
- jangan keluarkan benda asing
- imobilisasi mata

![[Pasted image 20260506180458.png|288]]

---
### 💉 Farmakologi

- profilaksis tetanus
- antibiotik spektrum luas
- rabies (jika gigitan hewan)
- salep/topikal antibiotik

---
### 🔧 Tindakan Bedah

Tujuan:

- rekonstruksi canaliculus
- mencegah epiphora

⏱️ Waktu ideal:

### Teknik:

- monocanalicular stent (Mini Monoka)
- bicanalicular stent (Crawford atau Ritleng)

---
### 🔄 Follow-up

- kontrol 1 minggu
- jahitan dilepas 10–14 hari
- stent dilepas:
    - mono: 8–12 minggu
    - bi: ±3 bulan

---
## ⚠️ Komplikasi

- epiphora kronik 💧
- infeksi
- stenosis canaliculus
- malposisi kelopak

# 3. 👁️ EYELID TRAUMA

## 📌 Klasifikasi

### Berdasarkan mekanisme:

- blunt trauma
- sharp trauma
- chemical burn
- thermal injury

### Berdasarkan berat:

- superficial laceration
- full thickness laceration
- avulsion
- tissue loss

## 📌 Clinical Evaluation

### 🗣️ Anamnesis

- Mekanisme trauma (blunt, sharp, chemical, thermal)
- Waktu kejadian
- Gejala penyerta:
    - nyeri
    - penurunan visus
    - diplopia
    - perdarahan

---
### 👁️ Pemeriksaan Fisik

#### 🔍 Pemeriksaan dasar mata:

- **Visual acuity** → wajib dulu (jangan sampai miss cedera bola mata)
- **Pupil (reaksi & ukuran)** → lihat kemungkinan cedera saraf optik
- **Gerakan bola mata (EOM)** → cek otot ekstraokular
- **Field of vision**

#### 🔍 Pemeriksaan lokal kelopak:

- inspeksi luka:
    - superficial vs full-thickness
    - ada tissue loss atau tidak
- cari:
    - benda asing
    - keterlibatan canaliculus (dekat medial canthus ⚠️)
- evaluasi fungsi:
    - **levator palpebrae** → ptosis?
    - **orbicularis oculi** → penutupan mata

---
### 🧪 Imaging

- **CT scan orbita**:
    - jika curiga fraktur
    - trauma berat
    - benda asing dalam
- **MRI**:
    - jaringan lunak (jarang di akut)

---
## 💊 Management

### 🚑 Initial Management

- hemostasis (hentikan perdarahan)
- irigasi luka (normal saline)
- bersihkan debris
- profilaksis tetanus

---
### 🔧 Surgical Management

#### 🔹 Prinsip utama:

👉 **layered closure (penjahitan berlapis sesuai anatomi)**

Kenapa penting?

- kelopak mata punya banyak lapisan:
    - kulit
    - otot (orbicularis)
    - tarsus
    - konjungtiva

---
### 🔹 Berdasarkan jenis cedera:

#### 1. Laceration biasa

- jahit primer
- perhatikan alignment margin kelopak
#### 2. Canalicular injury ⚠️

- wajib repair + stent
- kalau miss → epiphora kronik
#### 3. Avulsion / tissue loss

- flap / graft
#### 4. Burns (kimia/termal)

- irigasi segera
- cegah kontraktur

---
### 💊 Post-operative care

- antibiotik topikal / sistemik
- analgesik
- monitoring infeksi
- kontrol penyembuhan luka

---
## ⚠️ Complications

- ptosis
- lagophthalmos
- entropion / ectropion
- scarring
- notching
- obstruksi lakrimal

# 4. 🔥 DACRYOCYSTITIS

## 📌 Definisi

Inflamasi **saccus lacrimalis** akibat obstruksi ductus nasolacrimalis.

![[Pasted image 20260506181952.png|316]]
![[Pasted image 20260506181912.png|318]]

---
## 📌 Klasifikasi

- Congenital
- Acquired, terbagi dua: acute dan chronic

---
## ⚙️ Patofisiologi

Obstruksi duktus nasolakrimal (bisa karna benda asing, kongesti, fraktur atau tumor di sinus maksillaris, deviasi septum, polip → akumulasi air mata, debris epitelial, mukus di saccus lakrimal → bakteri berkembang → infeksi

There are 3 stages of secretion formation in dacryocystitis:
• **Obstruction Stage:** Excessive tears flow out.
• **Infection Stage:** Mucous, mucopurulent, or purulent fluid depending on the causative organism.
• **Scarring Stage:** No regurgitation of tears or pus anymore, as the secretions are retained in the sac, forming a cyst.

---
## 🧫 Bakteri umum

- Staphylococcus
- Streptococcus
- H. influenzae

---
## 🧠 Gejala

### Acute:

- nyeri tekan di area saccus lakrimal
- epiphora
- bengkak medial canthus
- demam
- discharge mukopurulen klo saccus lakrimal ditekan

### Chronic:

- tidak nyeri
- tanda2 inflamasi ringan
- epiphora
- kelopak saling menempel
- bisa discharge mukoid dengan pus di punctum
- Kadang ada fistula di permukaan lacrimal sac

### Congenital:

- Epiphora
- ada cairan dri punctum klo lacrimal sac ditekan
- bengkak di dasar hidung

---
## 🔬 Diagnosis

- Berdasarkan gejala klinis dan pemfis
- regurgitation test
- fluorescein dye disappearance test
- probing test and anel test for obstruction location
- CT / DCG (Dacrocystography)
- MRI

![[Pasted image 20260506183301.png]]

![[Pasted image 20260506183357.png]]

---
## 💊 Tatalaksana

### Anak:

- massage sac (Crigler)
- antibiotik
### Dewasa:

- kompres hangat
- antibiotik sistemik
- analgesik
- klo abses: insisi dan drainase

### Bedah:

- **DCR (dacryocystorhinostomy)** → gold standard (A direct connection is made between the lacrimal drainage system and the nasal cavity by bypassing the lacrimal sac)
- DCT/Dacryocystectomy (jika DCR tidak bisa)
    
---
## ⚠️ Komplikasi

- fistula saccus lakrimal
- ulkus kornea
- orbital cellulitis
- konjungtivitis rekuren

---
## Prognosis

Dacryocystitis is highly sensitive to antibiotics but can still relapse if the nasolacrimal duct obstruction is not treated properly. However, if the obstruction is properly addressed, the prognosis is good.

# 5. 🧿 DACRYOADENITIS

## 📌 Definisi

Inflamasi glandula lacrimalis, bisa akut atau kronik

![[Pasted image 20260506190953.png]]

---
## ⚡ Etiologi Singkat

- Infeksi (akut, biasanya viral: EBV)
- Autoimun (kronik, biasanya Sjögren's syndrome, sarcoidosis, atau tumor)
- Idiopatik

### Utk lebih detailnya:

Infectious Causes (more common in acute)
• Viral → Mumps (most common), Epstein-Barr virus (EBV), Herpes simplex virus, Cytomegalovirus
• Bacterial → Staphylococcus aureus, Streptococcus spp., Neisseria gonorrhoeae (rare)
• Fungal/Mycobacterial → immunocompromised patients

Non-infectious Causes
• Autoimmune → Sjogren’s syndrome, Sarcoidosis, Thyroid eye disease
• Neoplastic → Lymphoma, adenoid cystic carcinoma
• Idiopathic orbital inflammation (IOI)

---

## 🧠 Gejala

Akut:
- nyeri, bengkak
- pembengkakan superolateral
- S-shaped eyelid
- ptosis
- demam, malaise

Kronik:
- Bengkak tnpa nyeri
- Mata kering
- Bisa seperti tumor

---
## Pemeriksaan Fisis dan Penunjang

Physical Examinations:
➢ Inspection: Swelling, erythema, eyelid ptosis
➢ Palpation: Tenderness over lacrimal gland
➢ Ocular motility: May be limited in upward gaze

Supporting Examinations:
➢ CT/MRI: Enlargement of lacrimal gland, rule out abscess or tumor
➢ Lacrimal gland biopsy (for persistent / chronic cases)
➢ Labs: CBC, ESR, autoimmune panel, viral serology

---
## Diagnosis Banding

• Preseptal/orbital cellulitis
• Lacrimal gland tumors (e.g., pleomorphic adenoma)
• Idiopathic orbital inflammation (IOI)
• Sarcoidosis, Sjogren’s syndrome, Thyroid eye disease
## 💊 Tatalaksana

- viral akut → terapi suportif (kompres hangat, NSAID, istirahat)
- bakteri → antibiotik
- autoimun → steroid
- Klo abses: insisi dan drainase

---
## ⚠️ Komplikasi

- abses
- orbital cellulitis
- fibrosis
- Chronic autoimmune: Chronic dry eye due to gland damage or immunosuppressants
- Misdiagnosis of malignancy
- Fibrosis or gland atrophy

## Prognosis

• Acute viral: Excellent, self-limiting in 1-2 weeks
• Bacterial: Good if treated early
• Chronic: Variable depending on underlying cause
• Delayed treatment may result in scarring and dysfunction

# 6. 💧 DACRYOSTENOSIS (NLDO)

## 📌 Definisi

Obstruksi ductus nasolacrimalis (umumnya hanya menyebabkan epiphora, tpi klo infeksi bisa jdi dakriosistitis)

---
## 👶 Etiologi

- kongenital (gagal kanalisasi)
- dewasa: sinusitis kronik, infeksi, trauma, tumor

![[Pasted image 20260506191857.png]]

---
## 🧠 Gejala

- epiphora
- discharge mukopurulen
- konjungtivitis berulang
- Bengkak di lacrimal sac

---
## Diagnosis

Clinical history and physical exam
• Dye disappearance test (in children)
• Probing and irrigation
• Dacryocystography or dacryoscintigraphy (in adults)
• CT scan if tumor is suspected

---
## 💊 Tatalaksana

- bayi → massage (Crigler)
- Kebanyakan kasus sembuh spontan dalam 12 bulan
- probing (jika tdk sembuh dlam 12 bulan)
- dewasa → DCR (Dakriosistorinostomi = surgical creation of a new drainage pathway)
- Antibiotik klo infeksi
